Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about careers at GSR Grand Developers

We welcome applications from all qualified candidates. Whether you're an experienced professional looking for a new challenge or a fresher eager to start your career, we have opportunities for everyone. Each position specifies the required experience level.
Yes, absolutely! We actively hire freshers for positions like Telecaller, Sales Executive, and Digital Marketing Executive. We provide comprehensive training and mentorship programs to help you develop your skills. Hard work, enthusiasm, and willingness to learn are what matter most to us.
Typically, our recruitment process takes 2-3 weeks from the time you submit your application. This includes an initial review, phone interview, technical/practical assessment, and final interview with management. We'll keep you updated at each stage.
Stage 1: Phone/Video Screening - Introduction and brief discussion about your background
Stage 2: Technical/Practical Assessment - Role-specific test or presentation
Stage 3: Final Interview - Meeting with the hiring manager and senior management
Stage 4: Offer - If selected, we'll discuss terms and conditions
Salaries are competitive and based on your experience, qualifications, and role. We also offer performance-based incentives, bonuses, and other benefits. Specific salary details will be discussed during the interview process.
Most positions are based in Hosur, our primary office location. However, we're open to discussing flexible work arrangements for suitable roles. This will be clarified during the interview process.
